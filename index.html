Owned by the Mondelez International, Toblerone is a chocolate brand produced exclusively in Switzerland and Slovakia.

This trianglular chocolatey confection was the first milk chocolate bar ever to be patented, dating all the way back to 1909.

There are many different Toblerone variants, ranging from different flavors (milk, dark, white), sizes and products (ice cream, cake, etc). However, the main ingredients of Toblerone generally consist of chocolate, honey, and almond nougat.

About the company

Mondelez International was founded in Chicago in 1923, originally under the name "Kraft Foods Inc." The company was renamed to its current name in 2012. Mondelez International deals in the food and beverage business, manufacturing countless different brands of candy, confections, and sugary drinks.
